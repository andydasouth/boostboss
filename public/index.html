How It Works
Three steps. That's it.
Boost Boss connects advertisers to AI app users through MCP — the universal tool protocol for AI agents.
1

Developer adds Boost Boss

One MCP server URL + a tiny SDK component. Under 10 minutes to integrate. Your AI app now serves contextual ads.

2

Advertiser uploads campaigns

Upload video clips, images, headlines, and set your targeting — region, language, interest keywords. Set your budget and go live.

3

Boost Boss matches and delivers

Our MCP engine reads conversation context, matches the best ad, and delivers it as a popup, video, or native in-chat card. All tracked.

4

Everyone earns

Advertisers pay per impression or click. Developers earn 65% revenue share. Users see relevant, closeable ads. Data flows back to everyone.
